Our Story

More than nails —
it's wearable art.

Parlour Nail was born from a lifelong dream — to open a world-class nail studio in the heart of Parramatta. After more than two decades in the beauty industry, our founder brought that vision to life in August 2020.

We believe every nail is a blank canvas. Our technicians approach every set with the patience and precision of a fine artist — because that's exactly what nail design demands. Highly trained, genuinely passionate, and incredibly friendly.

From intricate custom art to classic french tips, from full acrylic sets to express shellac — we do it all with care and expertise that keeps our clients coming back.
